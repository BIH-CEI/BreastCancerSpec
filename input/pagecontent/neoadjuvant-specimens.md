### Neoadjuvant Therapy Resection Specimens

This scenario models a **post-neoadjuvant chemotherapy resection** specimen from a 48-year-old female patient (Sabine Schmidt).

#### Clinical Setting
- Triple-negative invasive breast carcinoma, right breast
- Neoadjuvant chemotherapy (EC-Paclitaxel) completed
- Mastectomy after neoadjuvant therapy

#### Specimen Hierarchy

| Level | Instance | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Part | Mastectomy specimen | Complete mastectomy, right breast |
| Block | Paraffinblock 01–05 | Paraffin-embedded tissue blocks (tumor bed + margins) |
| Slide | HE-Schnitte, IHC-Schnitte | H&E and immunohistochemistry sections |

#### Key Findings
- **Pre-treatment Histological Type:** Invasive carcinoma NST
- **Treatment Response:** Partial response with residual tumor
- **Residual Tumor Size:** Residual tumor bed assessment
- **RCB (Residual Cancer Burden):** Assessment documented
- **Margins:** Uninvolved
- **ypTNM staging**
